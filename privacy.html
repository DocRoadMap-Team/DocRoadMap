Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

The DocroadMap Chrome extension helps users with administrative procedures through an intuitive interface, a smart chatbot, an integrated calendar, and accessibility features.

Data collection and processing

DocroadMap does not collect or store any sensitive personal data without the user's explicit consent. The extension may interact with official website content to help users complete their tasks, but no data is stored or transmitted to unauthorized third parties.

Data used

  • Data entered in the chatbot to generate personalized process flows
  • Accessibility preferences (stored locally)
  • Calendar reminders and deadlines defined by the user

This data is used solely to improve the user's experience within the extension.

Third-party services

The extension may use external services such as OpenAI to provide answers to administrative questions via the chatbot. No personally identifiable information is ever transmitted.

Data storage

All navigation and preference data is stored locally in the user's browser. The extension does not use tracking or analytics scripts.
